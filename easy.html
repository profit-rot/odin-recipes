Easiest Cooking You'll Ever Do

What's easier than Ordering in?

The best recipe, in the world, whether your a world class chef or a single dude living alone in the middle of nowhere, is a recipe someone else uses to prepare a meal for you.

All you need is the internet, or a smart phone.

Ingredients

  • A computer or cell phone with internet acces
  • A credit or debit card, or some form of online currency.
  • A tip.

Instructions

  1. Open device with internet acces and open your favorite browsing app.
  2. Go to grubhub.com or seamless.com
  3. In the search bar for either of these websites, type in a favorite restaurant or a genre of food you desire.
  4. When you have found the desired food or restaurant, add food items from their menu, to put them into you virtual shopping cart.
  5. After adding all of your desired food, continue to the checkout.
  6. Choose your form of payment and purchase food items from your chosen restaurant.
  7. Don't forget to give a 20%, minimum, tip to the delivery person.
  8. Wait until you hear a knock on the door or your doorbell. When you do, to your front door and retrieve your purchased food from your delivery person.
  9. Enjoy!
