No-Bake Required

Don't get too baked, or you may make the mistake of baking these no-bake cookies!

Seriously, get baked and then make these no-bakes. The hardest thing to mess up, wich is perfect for stoners like you!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Milk
  • 1 stick (8 Tbs) Unsalted Butter
  • 1/4 Cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • 3 Cups Old-Fashioned Rolled Oats
  • 1 Cup Smooth Peanut Butter
  • 1 Tbs Pure Vanilla Extract
  • Large pinch of Kosher Salt

Instructional Steps

  1. Line a baking sheet with wax or parchment paper.
  2. In a medium sauce pan, over meadium heat, bring the milk, sugar, butter and cocoa to a boil, stirring occasionally. Let mixture boil for about a minute, then remove from heat.
  3. Add oats, peanut butter, vanilla and salt to mixture and stir to combine.
  4. Drop spoonfuls oc the cookie mixture onto the parchement or wax paper covered baking sheet and let sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes, or until hardened.
  5. Enjoy!
